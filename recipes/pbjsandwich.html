Peanut butter and jelly (or PBJ for short) sandwiches are a great way to get a quick snack at any time of day. They are a great snack to have when it's late at night or a quick lunch for work and school.

To make a PBJ sandwich first you will need to gether a few things.You will need each of the following.

  • peanut butter
  • jelly
  • bread
  • a knife
  • a towel
  • a plate

once you have gathered your supplies we can get started by doing the list below.
  1. using the knife, take a scoop of peanut butter and spread it evenly over one side of one slice of bread.
  2. set the bread (peanut butter side up) to the side and wipe off the knife with the towel.
  3. use the knife again, this time to take some jelly and spread it evenly over one side of one slice of bread.
  4. place the second slice of bread over the first so that the peanut butter and jelly are laying on top of one another
  5. enjoy
